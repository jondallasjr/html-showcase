Every workflow step generates requests that are tracked, assigned, and monitored for SLAs. The centralized Request Management system manages all handoffs between roles, monitors approval status, triggers notifications, and provides visibility into bottlenecks. Approval workflows can be configured as: Sequential/Cascading (Erin ‚Üí Jon ‚Üí Hugh), Concurrent (send to 3 people, any 1 approval passes), or Dependent (Approval 2 only after Approval 1 completes).

SYSTEM TRIGGER: Research Schedule

Research Schedule is the master calendar driving the entire Market Glance process. It tracks ~7,067 active documents and triggers Market Glance creation either by: (1) Due date approaching for scheduled quarterly publications, or (2) Major market change detected requiring immediate analysis.
