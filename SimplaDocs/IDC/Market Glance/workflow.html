SYSTEM TRIGGER: Research Schedule

Research Schedule is the master calendar driving the entire Market Glance process. It tracks ~7,067 active documents and triggers Market Glance creation either by: (1) Due date approaching for scheduled quarterly publications, or (2) Major market change detected requiring immediate analysis. The system automatically notifies the assigned analyst when a Market Glance is triggered.

User Management Limitations
Coda can't automate workspace users ‚Üî document people fields. Requires manual workarounds contradicting automation goals. Can't detect overloaded/unavailable resources affecting workload distribution.
Notification System Unresolved
MS Teams/Outlook integration challenges. Unclear: build custom solutions vs. use limited-functionality Coda packs? SLA notifications and escalations at risk without reliable delivery.
External Integration Dependencies
Heavy reliance on: BrandFetch API, Pub4.0, Active Directory synchronization, Enterprise ChatGPT API. No documented error handling for failed integrations or data sync conflicts.
Multiple Calendar Consolidation
Research Schedule + Tracker/Spending Guide Schedules + Custom Content Calendars need merging into single unified calendar. Data cleansing and normalization across 7,067+ documents required.
Performance & Scalability Unknown
Designed for 200 concurrent users tracking 7,000+ documents. Architectural uncertainty: single large doc vs. distributed approach? Python graphic generator already shows intermittent issues at prototype scale.
Aggressive Timeline & Capacity
End-of-January 2026 deadline with only 22 hrs/week development capacity (Erin + Hugh). Dec 12 decision: build clean vs. connect POCs. Risk of perpetuating prototype technical debt under timeline pressure.
