The Consultant's Radar: Problem Identification System

LEVEL 1
Surface Problems
Obvious issues people complain about. Inefficient processes, missed deadlines. Where task executioners operate.
LEVEL 2
System Problems
Underlying causes creating surface problems. Poor stakeholder alignment, conflicting priorities. Real consultant territory.
LEVEL 3
Strategic Problems
Fundamental misalignments most people don't see. Where premium rates are earned through strategic thinking.

4-Week Transformation Framework

WEEK 1
Problem Spotting
Develop your problem radar. Identify inefficiencies, unclear processes, and systemic issues.
WEEK 2
Root Cause Analysis
Ask "Why?" until you reach system-level causes. Think strategically, not tactically.
WEEK 3
Solution Architecture
Sketch 2-3 potential solutions for each problem. Focus on approach, not perfect details.
WEEK 4
Value Articulation
Quantify impact: time saved, costs avoided, risks mitigated. Connect thinking to outcomes.
