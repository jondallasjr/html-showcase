The Pattern That Repeats Every Year

April-June: Setup Phase - New financial year planning documents released, cautious spending, optimal relationship building time

July-September: Acceleration - Mid-year spending acceleration, framework activations surge, capability demonstration opportunities

October-December: Panic Phase - Underspend panic hits departments, rapid procurement decisions, direct awards surge

January-March: Harvest - Year-end spend acceleration, emergency procurement peaks, maximum opportunity capture
