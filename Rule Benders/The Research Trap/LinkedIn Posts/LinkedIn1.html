Here's the exact 3-tier framework I use to identify high-impact research sources in under 5 minutes:

TIER 1: DECISION-MAKER DIRECT

  • Executive interviews & insights
  • Government primary data
  • Industry leader perspectives
  • Direct stakeholder intelligence

TIER 2: EXPERT SYNTHESIS

  • McKinsey/BCG/Deloitte reports
  • Peer-reviewed academic studies
  • Industry association analysis
  • Regulatory body assessments

TIER 3: BACKGROUND CONTEXT

  • General news coverage
  • Social media sentiment
  • Public forum discussions
  • Historical trend data
🎯 The rule: Spend 70% of research time in Tier 1, 25% in Tier 2, 5% in Tier 3.

I've used this across £10+ billion in government programs. Every time, the insights that drove decisions came from Tier 1 sources.

Stop democratizing your attention. Some sources are objectively more valuable than others.
