Cuenca, Ecuador

The Dallas family guide to our next chapter — from Cape Town to the Andes. Everything we know, everything we've researched, everything that matters.

The Short Version

Cuenca is a UNESCO World Heritage colonial city in the Ecuadorian Andes. It uses the US dollar, sits in Eastern Time (UTC-5), was rated the safest city in South America in 2025, and has 8,000–10,000 English-speaking expats. Rent for a nice 2BR is $400–700/month. A family of three can live well on $2,400–3,200/month. Yoga classes cost $4–7. The symphony is free.

The Story

We're Jon, Jess, and Bodhi. We've been living in Cape Town, our possessions are in Chiang Mai, and we're moving to Cuenca. The timezone issue in Chiang Mai was brutal — Jon's 9-5 ET workday ran from 8pm to 4am local. In Cuenca, 9am Eastern is 9am local. That single fact changed everything.

This site is our research brought to life. Real photos, real prices, real assessments. No tourism-board language. This is what we actually know.
